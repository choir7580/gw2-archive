LI Power Daredevil

Power Daredevil is really fun to play. It is very easy to learn, however like most Thief builds, is hard to master. This build features a low-intensity (LI) variant.

It is very mobile, with mobility being a major part of the rotation. With Staff, it has heaps of cleave for a Thief.

It is the only Thief specialization that keeps the core profession mechanic Steal and thus has access to stolen skills such as Throw Magnetic Bomb and Detonate Plasma, which is massive utility for certain raid bosses.

Profession Mechanics

Steal
