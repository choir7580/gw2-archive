Power Dragonhunter

Power Dragonhunter is somewhat easy to learn, but really hard master especially at high ping due to the need of animation cancelling. Still, it is arguably one of the most fun specializations to play.

It has really good burst. There is decent ranged potential with Longbow. You are quite survivable due to your profession mechanics in addition to perks from being a Guardian.

It somewhat relies on teammates that can bring Resolution such as: Guardians, Mesmers with Staff-5 ( Chaos Storm), Renegades with Invocation x-1-x ( Spirit Boon), Catalysts.

Profession Mechanics

Virtues

Generally, just save them because they provide nice passive effects. Of course, activate them in situations where the actives offer good value.
  • Spear of Justice : Damage (do not greed)
  • Spear of Justice → Hunter's Verdict : Linear Pull, 150 Defiance Damage + ~22 Defiance Damage (across 1.5 seconds)
  • Wings of Resolve : Area Healing, Self Condition Cleanse, Mobility
  • Shield of Courage : Area Aegis, Conal Block

Greatsword

Level Ⅰ - Whirling Wrath, Auto-attack

  1. Whirling Wrath (or after third auto-attack)
  2. Auto-attack

Level Ⅱ - Symbol of Resolution

  1. ( Symbol of Resolution if have +) Whirling Wrath (or after third auto-attack)
  2. Symbol of Resolution if not used in above step
  3. Auto-attack

Level Ⅲ - Leap of Faith, Binding Blade

Only if confident in timing.
  1. ( Symbol of Resolution if have +) Whirling Wrath (or after third auto-attack)
  2. Symbol of Resolution if not used in above step
  3. Leap of Faith if no need mobility (+ Binding Blade if have and no need crowd control)
  4. Auto-attack

Crowd Control

  • Binding Blade → Pull : 150 Defiance Damage

Notes

  • Use Whirling Wrath and Binding Blade inside boss hitbox
  • Animation Cancel aftercast of Symbol of Resolution (when the Symbol is placed) and Binding Blade by stowing weapon
  • Symbol of Resolution + Whirling Wrath : Cleansing Bolts
  • Symbol of Resolution + Leap of Faith : Self Light Aura
  • Leap of Faith : Mobility, Self Healing
  • Binding Blade → Pull : Pull adds for cleave

Longbow

  2. Auto-attack

Level Ⅱ - Symbol of Energy, Deflecting Shot

  1. Symbol of Energy (+ Deflecting Shot if have and no need crowd control)
  2. True Shot (+ Hunter's Ward if have)
  3. Auto-attack
  4. True Shot (+ Hunter's Ward if have)
  5. Auto-attack

Level Ⅲ - Weapon Swap

Only if Weapon Swapping at the end.

  2. True Shot (+ Hunter's Ward if have)
  3. Auto-attack
  4. True Shot (+ Hunter's Ward if have)
  5. Auto-attack
  6. ( Deflecting Shot if have +) Symbol of Energy
  7. Weapon Swap

Burst Combo
