Condition Specter

Condition Specter is extremely easy to learn, but actually really hard to master. It is extremely fun and chill to play, whilst having tons of agency and skill expression.

It is impeccably survivable with Shadow Shroud. It is also completely ranged. However like most Thief builds, it lacks in cleave.

You can soft carry your team with the nice amount of Barrier and Healing you provide.
