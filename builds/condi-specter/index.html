Condition Specter

Overview

Condition Specter is extremely easy to learn, but actually really hard to master. It is extremely fun and chill to play, whilst having tons of agency and skill expression.

It is impeccably survivable with Shadow Shroud. It is also pretty much completely ranged. However like most Thief builds, it lacks in cleave.

You can soft carry your team with the nice amount of Barrier and Healing you provide.

  • Dawn's Repose : 100 Defiance Damage (across 1 seconds)
  • Siphon : 250 Defiance Damage (across 5 seconds)
  • Mind Shock : 150 Defiance Damage

  • Can Exit Shadow Shroud early if a slot skill is about to be available

Slot Skills

Level Ⅰ -

  1. Spider Venom and Skale Venom whenever available
    • If it aint ticking, it needs clicking!

Options

  • Well of Gloom : Area Healing, Mobility; Cripple
    • Lower cooldown
  • Skelk Venom : Area Healing
    • Stronger healing
    • Quicker healing
  • Prepare Thousand Needles → Thousand Needles : Area Conditions
    • 'Immobile' boss
  • Devourer Venom : Immobilize ( Poison)
    • 'Mobile' boss
  • Basilisk Venom : 150-750 Defiance Damage
  • Thieves Guild : Allies; Damage & Conditions

Niche

  • Prepare Shadow Portal → Shadow Portal : Portal; Stealth, Weakness
  • Shadowfall : 300 Defiance Damage, Mobility; Dark Field, Damage
    • Consistent CC

Notes

  • Since Spider Venom and Skale Venom are instant-cast, try to use them during any Skill Animation
  • Can precast Prepare Thousand Needles before encounter

Traits

Specter 3/1/2-1-1

  1. Consume Shadows : Area Healing &/ Barrier ∝ Shroud Time
    • Second Opinion : Healing Power, Healing Power ∝ Scepter & Condition Damage
    • Shallow Grave : Lethal Blow → Healing ∝ Shadow Force
  2. Larcenous Torment : ↑ Torment Damage, Shroud Gain = Torment
  3. Strength of Shadows : Expertise ∝ Vitality, ↑ Damage Reduced ∝ # Enemy Conditions, ↑ Torment Duration

Trickery 3-3/1-1

  1. Thrill of the Crime : Area Fury & Might & Swiftness = Siphon
  2. Pressure Striking : Torment = Interrupt
    • Bountiful Theft : Ally Area Boon Steal & Vigor = Siphon
  3. Quick Pockets : Initiative = Swapping & Entering/Exiting Shroud

Deadly Arts 3-2-1

  1. Panic Strike : Poison ∝ Immobilize, Immobilize = ↓ Enemy Health
  2. Deadly Ambition : Poison = Dual Attack, ↑ Condition Damage
  3. Potent Poison : ↑ Poison Damage & Duration, Poison Stacks ∝ Deadly Arts
